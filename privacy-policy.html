This Privacy Policy describes how DONIA LABS TECH ("we", "us", or "our"), operated by Daoud Touina, collects, uses, and protects information when you use our services at donialabstech.online, including the DONIA AI AI platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Business name, niche, location, and preferences you provide.
  • Facebook Page Data: Page ID, Page name, and Page Access Token to publish on your behalf. We do not access your personal Facebook profile.
  • Generated Content: AI-generated posts and weekly plans stored in your session.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics with no personal identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate AI-powered Facebook content tailored to your business.
  • To publish posts to your connected Facebook Page on your behalf.
  • To save client profiles for a better user experience.
  • To improve the quality of our AI agents.

3. Facebook Permissions

We request only the minimum permissions needed:

  • pages_show_list: To display your connected Pages.
  • pages_manage_posts: To publish and schedule posts.
  • pages_read_engagement: To optimize posting times.

We never sell or share your Facebook data. Revoke access anytime via Facebook App Settings.

4. Data Storage & Security

  • Client profiles are stored in a secure local database.
  • Facebook Access Tokens are stored securely and never exposed publicly.
  • We do not store your Facebook password or login credentials.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data while you use our services. You may request full deletion at any time — all data will be permanently removed within 30 days.

6. Third-Party Services

  • Meta (Facebook) Graph API — subject to Meta's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Gemini API — subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

7. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.
  • Revoke Facebook permissions from your Facebook settings.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
